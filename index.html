About Leslie

Leslie Awasom

CRNA · COO · Mentor

With over a decade of experience in healthcare as a CRNA and now serving as the COO at XSITE Capital and Co-founder of Vita Nova Ketamine & Wellness Clinic, Leslie has had the unique opportunity to combine clinical expertise with investment acumen.

His work focuses on helping healthcare professionals and other busy professionals build generational wealth through multifamily real estate investments. At XSITE, he's worked with diverse teams to bring ideas to life and ensure seamless operations across a growing portfolio.

His mission is direct: help professionals embark on their multifamily investment journey, mentoring them to achieve success in business and beyond. To date, he's helped over 30 professionals realize their dreams as entrepreneurs — and hundreds more take their first steps toward financial freedom.
